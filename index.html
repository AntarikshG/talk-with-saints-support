About the App

Talk with Saints-AI – Spiritual Wisdom & Guidance brings timeless teachings from Swami Sivananda, Swami Vivekananda, Maharishi Ramana, and Paramhansa Yogananda.

Explore quotes, lectures, and spiritual insights, and interact with an AI-powered chatbot trained on their works to support your spiritual journey.

Key Features

  • Quotes from great saints
  • Lectures and teachings on spirituality and self-realization
  • AI-powered spiritual chatbot
  • Daily guidance for mindfulness and growth
  • Search and explore wisdom by topic

Privacy & Data

We do not collect personal information. Conversations are processed only to generate responses and are not stored permanently.
